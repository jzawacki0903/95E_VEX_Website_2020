Team 95E's Robot

Picture Updated 1/8/2020

"Team 95E's robot can be considered a complex tray, with the roller intake mechanism attached to a one-bar lift instead of mounted directly to the tray. This mechanism allows us to reach our arms up to score in the small and medium towers easily and efficiently. Our tray consists of four separate stages, allowing us to hold a total of ten cubes. To score stacks, we utilize a tilting mechanism to push the tray to a perpendicular position, where the rollers on hinges are pushed outwards by the tray, allowing us to back up and drop the stack." -Jacob Zawacki 12/26/19
