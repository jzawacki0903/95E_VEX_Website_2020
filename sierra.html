Team 95S's Robot

Updated 1/8/2020

"Team 95S's robot is of the Simple Tray archetype, which uses a roller intake subsystem attached to a tray, which consists of two stages and can hold eight cubes. The tray is mounted to a tilter mechanism, which pushes the tray into a perpendicular position, where we can outtake and back up to score stacks. We can also overflow the tray into towers to score." -Ashley Hiller 12/29/19
