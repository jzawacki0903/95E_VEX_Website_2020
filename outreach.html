Our Community Outreach Programs:

The CEC TriBots organize and participate in many community outreach programs in order to get more people involved and aware of STEM opportunities.

Women in Engineering

The CEC TriBots hosts an event at each local high school called the Women in Engineering Program. This event is a way to raise awareness and to introduce more women to the field of engineering and other STEM careers. The CEC TriBots also participates in the Girl Powered Online Challenge, where participants must tell the story of women in their group, and encourage other women to join STEM clubs and careers.

CEC Awareness

While the Central Educational Center exists to provide opportunities and looks inside various careers, many new high school students are unaware of the amazing courses that the CEC offers. To spread awareness of the CEC and its various programs, teachers and representatives from the CEC will take a "field trip" to each local high school and display different classes that are available at the CEC.

Arnall STEM Night

Each year, representatives from the CEC TriBots will participate in a STEM night at Arnall Middle School. The purpose of this STEM night is to gain interest in the fields of Robotics and Engineering, as well as raise awareness for anyone looking to join the CEC TriBots in the future. The CEC TriBots bring along many small robots for people to test, as well as one of the teams' competition robots.
