Our Statistics

In this page, you will find various statistics for teams 95S, 95T, 95E, and 95M from this season.

Team 95S

The most successful CEC TriBots team this season so far, Team 95S has played a total of 27 matches with fifteen wins and twelve losses.

Team 95T

Team 95T has had a rough season, playing 25 matches, of which six were wins, two were ties, and seventeen were losses.

Team 95E

Team 95E has had a successful season winning fourteen of the 27 matches played, leaving thirteen losses.

Team 95M

The final team, Team 95M has not had the best of luck this season, with eight wins and 16 losses out of the 24 matches played.

***All charts last updated 1/13/2019***
