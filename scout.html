Scouting Sheet:

Our Story

Scouting is an important part of any VEX Robotics competition. Scouting helps your team know your teammates' and opponents' strengths and weaknesses, as well as determine a good alliance partner for the elimination rounds. To help our teams with scouting, we have created a scouting sheet (pictured below) to help organize our scouting. We have made a download link available as well, in order to help other teams make scouting as efficient as possible.
